Renew your licenceYou have chosen to have your ID documents verified by the your employer or prospective employer

Your current or prospective employer needs to verify three forms of ID and complete an ID verification form.

You will need to upload the ID verification form on the next page. You can upload the ID Verification form by taking a picture of it using a mobile or tablet device.

We do not require verified copies of the ID documents.

Criteria for ID documents

You must have at least 3 documents verified.

The documents must:.

  • be in your name (except your birth certificate, which is also acceptable in a different name)
  • be in your current address (where an address is included, at least 1 document should include this)
  • include your date of birth (at least 1 document should include this)
  • be original documents (you cannot use photocopies)

ID documents you can have verified

  • Driving licence (any current photocard or paper, provisional or full)
  • Passport (any current and valid)
  • Bank or building society statement (UK or EEA - issued in last 3 months)
  • Birth certificate (UK, Isle of Man and Channel Islands)
  • Utility bill, for example, water, gas or electricity, you cannot use mobile phone bills (UK - issued in last 3 months)
  • Credit card statement (UK or EEA - issued in last 3 months)
  • Council tax statement (UK - issued in last 12 months)
  • EU National ID card (Current and valid)
  • P45 (UK - issued in last 12 months)
  • Bank or building society account opening letter (UK - issued in last 3 months)
  • Financial statement, for example pension or endowment (UK - issued in last 12 months)
  • Benefit statement, for example pension or endowment (UK - issued in last 3 months)
  • Mortgage statement (UK or EEA - issued in last 12 months)
