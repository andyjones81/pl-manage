Choose a way to prove your identity

To maintain your licence , we must confirm your identity. You can do this either through your employer or using GOV.UK Verify.

If you use GOV.UK Verify, you can then use this to access other government services online.

Your employer

You can ask your employer to check and confirm original identity documents and complete an ID Verification form.

You will need to upload the completed form in the next step.

GOV.UK Verify

You can use GOV.UK verify and have your identity checked electronically.

If you have not used GOV.UK Verify before, it will take about 15 minutes to complete your ID check the first time. It works best if you have:

  • a UK address
  • a valid passport or photocard driving licence
