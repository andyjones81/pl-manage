Your privacy

The Gambling Commission (the Commission) is legally required, under the Data Protection Act 1998 to make sure you know what we intend to do with your information and who it will be shared with.

Our general approach is detailed in our privacy statement (link opens in a new window).

Your obligations

In accordance with Part 3 of the Licence Conditions and Codes of Practice (link opens in a new window), you must tell us within five working days, or as soon as you can, if any of the following events occur:

  • you change your name or address
  • an offence under Schedule 7 of the Gambling Act 2005 (link opens in a new window)
  • any investigations by any professional or statutory bodies in Great Britain or abroad
  • any sanctions or disciplinary action imposed on you
  • your disqualification from acting as a company director
  • your petition for bankruptcy, sequestration or entering into an Individual Voluntary Arrangement (IVA)
Do this online. You can report any events from your account by choosing 'Report an event'

Maintaining your licence

You need to maintain your licence every 5 years and pay a fee to cover the processing.

Do this online. We will notify you by email when this is due. It is important that you keep your email address up to date, otherwise you risk your licence being revoked.

Keeping your details up to date

In addition to telling us if your name or address changes, it is important that your contact details are also kept up to date.

Do this online. You can update your contact details through 'Manage your details' once in your account.
