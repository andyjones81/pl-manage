Change your name

To change your name you'll need to tell us your new name, when you started using it and provide some evidence.

Before you start

  • there is no fee payable to change your name
  • evidence can be a marriage certificate or deed poll
  • we will automatically create a key event for the change of name in accordance with Part III of the Licence Conditions and Codes of Practice (link opens in new window)

You can upload evidence using a mobile phone or tablet by taking a picture of your evidence document. We do not require originals to be posted to us.
