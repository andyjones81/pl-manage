Maintain your licence

To keep your {{accountData.type}} active, you need to maintain it every 5 years.

Your licence renewal is due by {{accountData.renewalDate | date('DD MMMM YYYY')}}

It costs £{{accountData.fee}} to maintain your licence, you must be paid by credit or debit card online. This fee is non-refundable.

! Warning We are unable to accept payment of this fee from anyone except you. We will reject your application if the payment does not meet these requirements.

To maintain your licence you'll need:

  • all your addresses for the last 5 years and the dates you lived there
  • a credit or debit card in your name to pay the renewal fee
  • details of ID documents such as your passport or drivers licence

If you have lived outside the UK in the last 5 years, you'll also need:

  • copies of police reports
  • copy of a credit report or letter of good standing from your bank.

Where these documents are not in English, you will need to provide originals and certified copies translated into English.
