What you must report

  • an investigation of an offence listed under Schedule 7 (Link opens in a new window)
  • a conviction of any offence listed under Schedule 7
  • any current or pending investigation by a professional, statutory, regulatory or government body in Great Britain or abroad
  • any sanction or penalty against you following an investigation by a professional, statutory, regulatory or government body in Great Britain or abroad
  • any sanction or dismissal for gross misconduct
  • your resignation from a position for which a personal licence is required following commencement of disciplinary proceedings in respect of gross misconduct
  • your disqualification from acting as a company director
  • your petition for bankruptcy, sequestration or entering into an individual voluntary agreement.

You will need to raise separate events if you have multiple things to tell us.
